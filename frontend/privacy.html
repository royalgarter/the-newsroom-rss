Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

At NewsRSS (https://newsrss.org), accessible from https://newsrss.org, your privacy is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by NewsRSS and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to ensure the best user experience. This includes:

  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personally identifiable information such as browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited on our site. This helps us understand how visitors use NewsRSS.
  • Personal Data: If you create an account, subscribe to a newsletter, or contact us, we may ask for your name, email address, and other relevant details. We only collect this when voluntarily submitted by you.
  • Local Storage: If you use any customizable features on our site, we may use your browser's local storage to save your feed preferences on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our platform.
  • Understand and analyze how you use NewsRSS.
  • Communicate with you for customer service, updates, and site-related information.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, NewsRSS uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

NewsRSS provides links to third-party RSS feeds, directories, and news sources. Once you click on these links or load external feed content, you are subject to the privacy policies of those third parties. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

5. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we have on you. If you wish to be informed about what Personal Data we hold about you or if you want it to be removed from our systems, please contact us.

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: privacy@newsrss.org.
